FAQs

Common Questions

How quickly can I reserve a goat? ▾

Reservations are instant through WhatsApp. Click the Reserve button on any listing, and our team will confirm within minutes during business hours (6 AM – 8 PM).

What does "live weight" mean for pricing? ▾

Live weight is the weight of the goat when it's alive — before slaughter. Pricing is listed per kg of live body weight, so a 30 kg goat at ₹620/kg costs ₹18,600 total.

How do I sell my goats through Protein Eaters? ▾

Fill out the farmer submission form on our For Farmers page or WhatsApp us directly. We'll contact you within 24 hours to get you set up.

Do you deliver goats? ▾

Yes, we help coordinate transport and delivery across Tamil Nadu. Delivery charges depend on quantity and distance. Contact us to discuss logistics for your order.

What breeds do you typically have available? ▾

We regularly source Kanni Adu, Salem Black, Mecheri, Tellicherry, Boer Cross, and Osmanabadi breeds from across Tamil Nadu. Availability varies daily — contact us for current stock.
